
Evan:

**San-ta**? ↗

/ **San-ta** is **Chi-nese**. ↘

Santa:

**Yes.** ↘

Evan:

but / you’re a **wo-man**. ↘

Santa:

**Yes**, ↘

Evan:

then / **why** do you have a **beard**? ↗

Santa:

This is **mole hair**. ↘

It all grows out of / two very **lu-cky moles**. ↘

Evan:

I’ve **ne-ver** heard of **wo-men**, / **San-ta**. ↘

Santa:

You **think** a **man** is **thought-ful** enough /

to give **pre-sents** to **e-very-one** in the **world**? ↗

Do you **think** a **man** is **or-ga-nized** enough /

to make a **list** /

of the **be-ha-vior** /

of **e-very** sin-gle **boy** and **girl**? ↗

You **know** / those are **street signs** /

that say / **men at work**. ↘

**Wo-men** don’t need those /

be-cause / it is **un-der-stood**. ↘

Evan:

but / all the **San-tas** I ever **see** /

are **white men**. ↘

Santa:

They all work for **me**. ↘

I am the **whole-sa-ler**. ↘

They are my **mid-dle-men**. ↘

Evan:

But / **why** are you **here** to-night? ↗

It’s not **Christ-mas Eve** yet. ↘

Santa:

I come on De-cem-ber **twen-ty-third**, ↘

and I **hide** the **pre-sents**. ↘

Then my white **San-tas** come **Christ-mas Eve** ↘

and put the **pre-sents** out un-der the **tree**. ↘

I al-low them to take **cre-dit**, ↘

but / **I** am the **boss**. ↘

This is why I am called **lao ban San-ta**. ↘

Evan:

**Boss San-ta**? ↗

Santa:

That’s **right**. ↘

I **bet-ter go**. ↘

My **La-ba-ron** is i-dling in the **drive-way**. ↘

Evan:

You drive a **La-ba-ron**? ↗

Santa:

It’s a **lease** /

bet-ter for **tax pur-po-ses**. ↘

You must **pro-mise** /

**ne-ver** to tell **an-y-one** a-bout **me**, ↘

not e-ven your **bro-thers**. ↘

You are a **spe-cial soul**. ↘

one who be-**lieves** in **ma-gic** /

but still has a **sci-en-ti-fic mind** /

like **me**. ↘
