# 2. Link Layer Protocols

$$
U = \frac{L/R}{L/R + 2T_p} \ge 0.5 \Rightarrow L \ge 2T_p R
$$

$$
L \ge 2 \times 0.02 \times 8000 = 320 \text{ bits} = 40 \text{ bytes}
$$

So frame size ≥ **40 bytes**
